Help & FAQs

How do I find the train I need?

You can quickly navigate to the 'Choose your train' section on the main page by selecting it from the slide out menu (opened with the menu button in the top left of the screen).

This section allows you to access the timetable (provided by Metrorail) for every station in the Cape Town rail system.

First Select a Line, then Select a Station, the select your Direction of Travel.

Your selected station will be displayed below along with the timetable.

You will then be able to choose the train that has an Expected Arrival Time that best suites you.

How do I find out where my train is?

Once you have finished the first step of Choosing your train(as explained above), the 'Track your train' section below will be populated with a feed displaying the time and station at which your selected train was last spotted.

How do I report when I see a train?

Reporting any train you spot, no matter the direction it's travelling, simply navigate to the 'Share train location' section, selct the Station you are at and the train you have just spotted. The number of the train is displayed on the front and back ends of it in red lights so keep a lookout as the train enters and/or leaves the station.

You are also able to fill out the optional Name field if you would like your name to accompany your update in to the train feed. This will allow you to see how many people appreciated your post and marked it as 'helpful'.
