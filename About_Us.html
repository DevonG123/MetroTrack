About Us

Welcome to MetroTrack, your one-stop website for reliable train location updates from the commuting public.

We’ve heard your calls for a more reliable public transport system, so we’re offering to be the ‘middleman’.

We’re calling on you to post updates via this website on the whereabouts of any trains you spot. We'll take care of making your simple post help as many of your fellow commuters as possible.

In return you get access to train location updates made by the rest of the MetroTrack community from train stations throughout the City of Cape Town.

Let’s help each other as we commute through this beautiful city.
